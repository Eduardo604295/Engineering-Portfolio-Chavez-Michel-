Shock Assembly Design

Designed and assembled a complete mechanical shock absorber system in SOLIDWORKS, emphasizing assembly modeling, component integration, engineering drawings, and proper mechanical constraints. The project demonstrates proficiency in CAD assembly design and technical documentation.

Project Overview

This project involved designing and assembling a complete shock absorber system in SOLIDWORKS with attention to component fit, assembly constraints, and technical documentation. The final assembly includes a shock body, spring, and fastening elements organized into a manufacturable CAD model. Engineering drawings and visual presentation materials were developed to communicate assembly strategy, component relationships, and final design quality.
